Home Inventory Manager

Organize your life, one item at a time. A comprehensive solution for tracking personal belongings for insurance claims, moving, and peace of mind.

The Challenge

An AI-powered application designed to store, organize, and reduce wastage. It offers smart suggestions and a comprehensive feature set to manage your household inventory efficiently.

The challenge was to create a frictionless mobile experience that encourages users to catalog their items quickly, providing peace of mind without the administrative burden.

Key Features

qr_code_scanner

Barcode Scanning

Instantly populate item details by scanning barcodes, leveraging an external product database API.

cloud_upload

Cloud Sync

Securely backup inventory data to the cloud, ensuring access from multiple devices and safe recovery.

receipt_long

Export Reports

Generate PDF or CSV reports for insurance claims instantly, complete with photos and estimated values.

notification_important

Expiry Alerts

Set reminders for warranty expirations or perishable items to keep your home managed efficiently.
